Key Features

Display Tasks Lists

User-defined tasks are displayed in cards that make them easy to view.

Edit Tasks

Users are able to edit existing tasks to update their state, change their name, or other relevant details.

Sort Tasks

Users can sort their tasks based on priority (ascending or descending) or due date.

About Swiftly Tasks

This is Swiftly Tasks, a portfolio project to conclude the foundations phase at ALX.
The aim of this project is to demonstrate key compotencies in both frontend and backend development
which has been cultivated during the initial 9 months of the program.

Swiftly Tasks has been developed by Keitumetse Molefe and Jordan Williams of Cohort 14.

Working in teams throughout the year has highlighted the importance of cohesion and synergy among members
in order to achieve a common goal. Having clearly defined tasks and priorities set for each individual
increases productivity and gives each team member a definitive path towards goal-attainment.
This has been the driving force behind the development of Swiftly Tasks, our task management app.
